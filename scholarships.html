About One Zambia Youth Empowerment Programme Scholarships

Don't miss the 100% Scholarships offered by One Zambia Youth Empowerment Programme in Partnership with Melburn Institute of Applied Science and Sunningdale University.

One Zambia Youth Empowerment Progamme is a programme aimed at empowering youths with qualifications and skills by providing grade 7, 9, and 12, 100% scholarships in Certificate, Diploma and Bachelors degree at Sunningdale University and Melburn Institute of Applied Sciences in Full courses and Short courses.

How to apply for scholarship

In order to apply for this scholarship, an application form has to be purchased at a price of k200 for Full courses and k100 for Short courses by depositing the fee. The form can be purchased by contacting the bursaries coordinator. Upon filling and submision of the scholarship form an acceptance is issued after due processing.
